About Me

My name is Malachi White. I am 22 years old, I was born in Portland, Oregon, and I was raised in Tulsa, Oklahoma when my family moved there when I was 9 ½ years old. I am currently a Junior studying Computer Information Systems. Growing up, I played basketball religiously; my dad played basketball, my uncle played in college, my brothers play and love basketball, I have cousins who played in the NBA, and quite frankly, I was a good player. So naturally, I wanted to go to the NBA. I ended up going to college in Louisiana for basketball, it didn’t entirely turn out as I had planned, so I left after a year. Fast forward to when Covid-19 struck, I walked away from the game I have loved all my life and immersed myself on a new path, coding! I started coding nearly four years ago and enjoyed the problem-solving skills needed to code. I was so intrigued by how everything has to work together to become something people use daily. I enjoyed the critical thinking aspect of using data structures and algorithms. Since I began, I have seen different ways coding and computers interact with the real world. I have a strong understanding of using Python, HTML & CSS, C++, JavaScript, Three.js, and Figma.
